# **App Name**: SIRAJ.IO — Siraj Platform

## Core Features:

- Immersive Course Player: An elegant, distraction-free UI for video lessons, textual explanations, and modular progress tracking.
- AI Academic Assistant: An AI-powered tool acting as a private tutor to help students clarify complex topics within their courses.
- Verified Certificate Hub: A centralized database system for issuing and validating professional credentials using unique serial numbers and Firestore storage.
- Strategic Learning Paths: Curated roadmaps that logically sequence multiple courses toward specific professional outcomes or job titles.
- Physical Book Library: An integrated marketplace UI for browsing and purchasing high-end educational physical textbooks.
- Unified Command Dashboard: Tailored analytics and management interfaces for both students (learning progress) and admins (order management).
- QR Verification Portal: A quick-scan utility to instantly authenticate physical certificate validity via camera integration.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Rich Cocoa (#4A1F0F), evoking high-end professionalism and grounded authority.
- Background Color: Ivory Cream (#F8F5EF), providing a warm, scholarly atmosphere that is soft on the eyes.
- Accent Color: Solar Gold (#D98A1E), used for active states and critical CTAs to symbolize enlightenment and energy.
- The 'Poppins' sans-serif font provides a modern, fashionable aesthetic for headers. Note: 'Cairo' and 'Alexandria' are recommended for high-quality Arabic support, though currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- A 'Minimal Luxury' layout featuring clean spacing, generous white space, and soft rounded card corners (14px) reminiscent of modern premium SaaS interfaces.
- Utilization of thin-stroke Lucide or Heroicons for a minimalist, non-distracting visual language that feels light and professional.
- Micro-interactions focused on smooth, low-duration transitions and soft glow effects on hover to emphasize luxury without feeling neon or futuristic.
